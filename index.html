How to Play
1. Press `Arrow Keys (←→↓↑)` or `WASD` to control the direction of the character.
2. Collect clock to increase time; collect lamp oil to extend vision.
3. All players need to reach the exit gate to win the game within time limit.

Tips
1. You can only see areas within your light scope.
2. Try to find other lamplighters!
3. Try to explore the map more. There is always a surprise elsewhere.
